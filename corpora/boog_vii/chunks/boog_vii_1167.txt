Once again, the Allies were suffering from supply bottlenecks, and in addition were now proceeding to cegroup their units. As from 15 September, the DRAGOON forces were placed directly under SHAEF. Lt .- General Jacob L. Devers formed the US 6th Army Group, which included Patch's US 7th Army and De Lattre de Tassigny's French Ist Army. Although this regrouping created a moment of weakness for the Allies, what was more serious was that Blaskowitz and his command managed relatively quickly to restock the many decimated German units with equipment and personnel and to redeploy them.
