He would also have to choose one of these men to come home and run the Air Forces as his deputy because his doctors forbade him to resume a full schedule. And he was going to have to send Barney Giles to the Pacific to replace Miff Harmon, whose plane had just disappeared on a routine flight, Barney hadn't yet been given his tour of duty in the field, and it was unfair to deprive him of it.
