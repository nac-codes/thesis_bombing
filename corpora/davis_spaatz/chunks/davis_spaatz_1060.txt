The Army "green series," published by the Office of the Chief of Military History (OCMH), now the Center of Military History (CMH), and produced by the U.S. Government Printing Office (GPO), supplies information on air-ground support, technical aspects of procurement, and prewar planning. Pogue's volume on the Supreme Command, Matloff's two volumes on Strategic Planning, and Leighton's and Coakley's two volumes on logistics provide essential information on high-level planning and give adequate treatment to air issues.
