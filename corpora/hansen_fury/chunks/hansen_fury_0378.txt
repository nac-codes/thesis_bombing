The Eighth Air Force would have four commands: Bomber and Fighter Commands, as well as Ground-Air Support Command and Air Service Command (responsible for supply and maintenance). Eighth Bomber Command was organized into combat wings, which were in turn comprised of three bomb groups (made up of squadrons), which went together on air raids.
