The blitz was another instance of the German capacity for making every possible big mistake and no small ones. After the almost incredible folly they showed in the Battle of Britain they at last saw the hopelessness of facing up to our fighters in daylight and said, “Well, we will do what the British do and bomb by night.” This was the final disastrous mistake they made in the air campaign against Britain, and they never recovered from it.
