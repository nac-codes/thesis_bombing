Faber  served as a professor of airpower history at SAAS from 1992 to 1994. After completing his undergraduate work at UCLA in 1976, he went on to receive graduate degrees from the University of Arkansas and the University of Alabama, and is completing his PhD at Yale University, under the guidance of Sir Michael Howard and Paul Kennedy. He is the author of numerous articles on airpower history and theory, and in 1989 was chosen as the outstanding instructor in the History Department at the US Air Force Academy.
