The military machine that made those conquests had been trained by some of the finest tactical and operational military minds in the history of warfare. Yet, even in the French campaign, a serious attrition of Germany's air and mechanized forces took place. For the next year and a quarter, the Wehrmacht went on a rampage that saw it conquer the Balkans, drive into Egypt, and win devastating victories in the Soviet Union.
